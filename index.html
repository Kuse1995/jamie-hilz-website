About the Milliner

Jamie Hilz crafts bespoke headwear that marries timeless silhouettes with modern lines — pieces that elevate the outfit and photograph beautifully. Every commission begins with a conversation about your event, outfit and personal style. Materials are selected for season and comfort, then shaped, stitched and trimmed by hand to fit you perfectly.

  • Custom-made hats, hatinators & hair accessories
  • Design consultation to match your outfit & event
  • Premium materials: straw, felt/wool, sinamay, veiling

The Process

Every hat is handcrafted in three considered stages for a couture result.

1 · Consultation

Share your event, outfit and inspiration. We discuss silhouette, proportion and palette.

2 · Design & Fit

Materials are selected for season and comfort. Blocks and trims are refined to your measurements.

3 · Finishing Touches

Hand‑stitched details, veiling and balance checks. Your piece is boxed and ready for the day.
